Summit is an app for Lemmy that enables you to explore hundreds of communities with ease.

Summit is optimized for Android and offers a smooth browsing experience.

Features
* An edge-to-edge browsing experience
* Upvote, comment and create content
* Easy to learn gesture navigation
* Seamless multi-account support
* Browse history
* Light/Dark and Material You themes
* Customize how posts look with dozens of unique configurations
* User inboxes
* View user profiles
* Customize web settings
* Swipe gesture actions
* Keyword and advanced filters
* View community and instance stats and descriptions
* Save posts & comments
* Multi-communities (grouping multiple communities together into one feed)
* Moderation & admin actions
* And much more to come!
